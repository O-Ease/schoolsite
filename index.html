WELCOME TO OUR SCHOOL

AIMS AND VISION

Ease World Secondary School is exclusively a day co-educational institution. The concept of Ease world is based on the fact that private participation in secondary school education is the only way to supplement the goverment's efforts in the country. Ease world secondary school not only provide a balance education which prepare students to meet the challenges of university life, but plays a unique role in preparing children today a successful tommorow. The vision of the school is based on the interaction and intergration of the male and famale child in discipline enviroment, established to promote a healthy competition, leadership opportunities and academic excellence.
